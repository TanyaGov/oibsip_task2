Tribute to Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi, born Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, was an Indian lawyer, anti-colonial nationalist, and political ethicist who employed nonviolent resistance to lead the successful campaign for India's independence from British rule.

What I Adore About Mahatma Gandhi

Gandhi's commitment to nonviolence and his unwavering dedication to civil rights and freedom are truly inspiring. His philosophy of Satyagraha, or truth and non-violence, has influenced countless movements for civil rights and freedom across the world.

Gandhi's simple lifestyle and his focus on self-reliance and community cooperation demonstrate his belief in living with humility and integrity. His teachings continue to resonate and inspire people to this day.
